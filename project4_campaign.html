Key Insights & Recommendations
Data-driven actions taken based on campaign analysis
✅
Retargeting delivers 4.2× ROAS — scale budget allocation
Site visitor retargeting campaigns consistently outperform cold audience campaigns across CTR, CPC, and CPA. Recommended reallocating 20% of broad-interest spend toward retargeting pools, saving an estimated $6.20 per acquisition.
⚠️
Broad Interest campaigns underperform — reduce or restructure
CPA of $68.40 on broad LV interest targeting is 40% above account average. Analysis showed high CPM with low intent signals. Recommended tightening to 35–65 homeowner demographic overlays to improve efficiency.
🔐
Meta Custom Audiences compliance — PII handling verified
Conducted compliance review of customer list upload procedures. Confirmed SHA-256 hashing of email and phone data prior to upload per Meta's data policy. Documented process for SOC audit trail and team SOP.
📍
Geographic targeting refine — ZIP-code overlap with territory data
Cross-referenced ad targeting geographies against the sales territory ZIP database. Identified 12 high-penetration ZIPs being excluded from ad targeting — fixing this alone increased impressions in served territory by 14%.
